How we met

I can't recall the exact day I met Ayobamidele, but I sure do remember taking notice of a heavenly voice sing 'The First Noel' during a carol rehearsal in one of Babcock Business School's Auditorium in the winter of 2014. I just wanted to know more. As fate would have it, a few days later, Ayobamidele met me on my way back to my hostel on a beautiful friday afternoon and invited me for Philadelphia's choir concert. I got back to my room and told my best man that I had found the woman of my dreams. Few months later, I was going on one knee in front of Babcock Business School and asking her to be my girlfriend and the rest they say is history.

Friday, 6th August

Church Service 10AM - 12PM

The christian service will be held at Foursquare Church, Olambe, Ogun State. The service will be in the presence of God, close family members, friends and church members. The joining is significant as we are both christians and believe in the utmost importance of joining before the body of Christ (The Church).

Saturday, 7th August

Traditional Engagement & Reception 10AM-6PM

This is the main ceremony where the traditional wedding rites according to the Yoruba culture is carried out. The dowry of the bride is paid and the family of the bride officially blesses the union and hands over their daughter to the care of the groom and his family. The union is blessed by family members from both sides. Food, drinks and dance follows immediately after the engagement ceremony
